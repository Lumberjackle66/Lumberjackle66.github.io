The Top 3 Teams Of The NA LCS

3) Cloud9 - 9 Wins 5 Losses

2) Counter Logic Gaming - 10 Wins 4 Losses

1) Immortals - 13 Wins 1 Loss

Immortals

The Immortals are by far the best team and will probably come first but everyone is looking ahead to worlds and wondering, "Do they have a chance?". Their surprising first loss to CLG means that "The Streak" has ended, especially for Huni and Reignover.

CLG

CLG are in a good position right now sitting in 2nd place. They currently are one win ahead of Cloud 9 but that shouldn't change unless CLG make a big mistake. There last four games are pretty simple because the only "challenge" they will face is Echo Fox and Team Liquid but they are expected to easily take them down. They are fighting for their 2nd place spot but when the season is this close to ending even small mistakes can cost them.
