Wildturtle Has The Most Kills

Wildturtle is continuing to rack up the kills and at the moment he has an astonishing 82 kills in just 14 games. Jensen has the second most kills but isn't even close with just 66 kills. WIldTurtle has been a big component to his team's success.

Adrian Has The Most Assists

Adrian is dominating the assists leaderboard with 164 assists. Part of that is because he is a support but many would call him a, "Team Player" because he only has 3 kills. He is well known for his Janna and Soraka having played one of two in 13 out of 14 of his games.
