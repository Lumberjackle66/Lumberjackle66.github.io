Emperor Has The Most Kills

Emperor is continuing to rack up the kills and at the moment he has an astonishing 66 kills in just 14 games. Steelback has the second most kills and is close with 66 kills. Emperor has been a big component to his team's success.

Hybrid Has The Most Assists

Hybrid is dominating the assists leaderboard with 145 assists. Part of that is because he is a support but many would call him a, "Team Player" because he only has 8 kills. He is well known for his Braum having played him in 8 out of 14 of his games.
